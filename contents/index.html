Hi! I am Gail Weiss, a researcher interested in understanding sequential neural networks (such as RNNs and transformers) through the lens of formal language theory, whether in order to extract more interpretable models (such as deterministic finite automata from RNNs), or understand what such interpretable models should even be (as in the case of transformers).
I did my postdoc at the EPFL NLP lab of Prof. Antoine Bosselut, and my PhD at the Technion under the guidance of Prof. Eran Yahav and Prof. Yoav Goldberg. I am currently doing private things :).

Outside of computer science, my interests include baking, drawing, and chasing pigeons off my windowsill. For a glorious few years, I ran the kitchen for the Technion CS faculty Mimuna—the end of Passover celebration—during which various helpful students and I made hundreds of mufleta for all of our students, faculty, and staff.

I am reachable at gail dot weiss dot research at gmail dot com.
